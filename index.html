The GILTY list

Games I Liked This Year

The GILTY list is a series of what some may generously refer to as "essays" on select games that I...liked this year. These essays serve more as a long winded explanation as to how they earned a spot on this coveted list than anything else. These are not "game reviews", heck they're hardly critiques. If it's game review scores you're after then check out my Arbitrary Super Scoring Machine. The official games review software of Yahoo's Echo Chamber.
